Place 1

These rules are in place along with all of the Roblox Community Rules These rules will be enforced more harshly than our rules They can be found here (Both player and developer rules must be followed)

  1. No Abusive Killing

    Abusive killing ruins the fun for everyone else. We want to maintain a usable environment for everyone in Place 1. Examples include, but are not limited to:

    • Spawnkilling
    • Rapidly killing people
    • Killing a lot of people with one key-press/click
    • Respawning too many people
    • Intentionally killing someone who wishes to not be killed
  2. No spamming

    Spamming makes annoys the other players, causes lag, or just makes the game unplayable for everyone else. Examples include but are not limited to:

    • Spamming server/global chat
    • Spamming objects created with Instance.new
    • Spamming get commands
    • Spamming any changes in object properties
  3. Don't attempt to bypass sandbox restrictions

    We have a sandbox for a reason. You should not try to bypass it in order to break rules. Includes but is not limited to:

    • Removing other players from the game
    • Bypassing Instance.new ratelimits
    • Bypassing g/nosound
    • Visible swearing
    • Trying to run blocked scripts
  4. Don't play obnoxious/inappropriate sounds

    We must maintain an environment where people don't have to adjust their volume, and maintain roblox's community standards. Includes but is not limited to:

    • Bypassed audio
    • "Ear Rape"
    • Extremely low bass
    • Repeatedly playing short sounds
  5. Do not backtalk staff
    • Any discrepancy with staff should be expressed in a calm and professional manner.
    • Do not be rude to staff
    • Do not retaliate to actions taken by staff
    • Do not try to pick fights with staff
    • If you believe staff to be wrong in a specific case, please record evidence, and then speak with an Administrator
  6. Do not evade punishment

    Evading punishment is not fair to everyone else, and it will be met with a more extreme punishment (minimum 2 month ban). Includes but is not limited to:

    • Rejoining the game with an alternate account
    • Leaving the game before the staff member can punish/warn you.
    • Leaving the game because official Void SB staff joined
  7. Other forms of abuse
    • Do not alter the appearance of other people's character
    • Do not prevent more than one person from moving at a time (assuming you have that person's permission to do so)

Tag Guidelines

  1. Do not use the tag system to impersonate staff members, or make it appear so that you are a member of the staff team
  2. Do not make any inappropriate/nsfw tags
  3. Do not have tags that could insinuate something immoral, illegal, or against any of the rules
  4. Do not violate any terms of the roblox community rules using the tag system
  5. Do not have tags containing any illegal content

Violating these rules may result in you being blacklisted, or the tag removed. If you are blacklisted, you will not receive a refund.

Place 1 Require Guidelines

  1. Do not allow other people to use require
  2. Do not force scripts onto other people, unless it is necessary for core functionality of the module
  3. Do not force invasive or awful looking UI on other people
  4. Do not remove anyone from the game
  5. Your module must follow all rules/guidelines for Place 1
  6. Do not mess with anything built into the script builder (including the chat system)
  7. Do not prevent others from executing scripts
  8. Do not modify any proprietary or CoreGUI in Void SB for other people
  9. Do not attempt to alter or exploit the sb

    10. Note: Only sb moderators+ may break rules 4 and 7 for moderation purposes

SB Staff Guidelines

  1. For small scale abuses, warn for the first offense(example, spawnkilling/killing without permission)
  2. For large scale abuses, you may immediately ban (example, server destruction, loopkilling everyone, unfiltered chats that are inappropriate)
  3. NSFW scripts are an immediate ban
  4. Roblox community guidelines violations may be an immediate ban (unless the player is ignorant that he/she is breaking them)
  5. Do not fight with other players or staff in the game, or discord server
  6. Staff is not allowed to save scripts that were logged
  7. Do not ban someone just because you don't like them (if they're being rude, or trying to pick a fight with you, then it is fine to ban for sass)
  8. Only ban for valid reasons
  9. Do not make up rules on the spot
  10. You must read any appeal relating to any ban carried out by you.
  11. You must be active in moderating the sb
  12. Follow every rule that applies to you
  13. Do not cause other players to break rules
  14. You are representing Void script builder. You are a role model to other players, you must act professional, and maintain a good image (especially on profiles viewed by sb players).
